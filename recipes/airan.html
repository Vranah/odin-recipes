Ayran

Description:

Ayran is a cold savory yogurt-based beverage that is consumed across Central Asia, and the Balkans, in Turkey and Iran. The principal ingredients are yogurt, water and salt. Herbs such as mint may be optionally added. Some varieties are carbonated.

Ingridients:

  • Plain Bulgarian Yogurt
  • Cold Water
  • Salt

Optional:

  • Mint
  • Sparkling Water
  • Cucumber

Steps:

  1. Place yogurt in a large bowl or measuring cup and whisk until it is smooth and free of lumps
  2. Add water and salt to the bowl and whisk vigorously until combined. Taste for seasoning and adjust if necessary. Alternatively, you can put all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until fully combined.
  3. Tightly cover your yogurt milk drink in a glass bottle with a lid (or stretch film). Store in the fridge until ready to serve or for up to 2 days.
  4. Give it a good shake before pouring it into glasses, and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired
